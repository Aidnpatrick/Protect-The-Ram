# Protect The RAM

**Protect The RAM** is a strategy/tower defense game where you defend your RAM from waves of AI robots.

---

## How to Play

1. Build your defenses  
2. Start the round  
3. Defend your RAM and eliminate enemies  
4. Earn money  
5. Repeat and improve your setup  

*A more detailed tutorial is available inside the game.*

---

## Buildings & Turrets

### Basic Turret
A balanced, all-purpose defense. Slightly weak, but reliable in most situations.

### Heavy Turret
Slow fire rate, but deals very high damage per shot.

### Poison Turret
Low damage, but slows enemies and temporarily prevents them from attacking.

### Machine Turret
Very fast fire rate with low damage. Ideal for handling large groups.

### Missile Turret
Launches missiles that deal high area damage. Very slow fire rate.

### Army Camp
Spawns a troop every few seconds. Useful for distracting enemies.

### Gold Digger
Generates income after each round. Can only be placed on deposit tiles (tiles with black markings).

### Boogie Mine
A landmine that forces nearby enemies to dance temporarily when triggered.

### Healing Tower
Heals nearby turrets within a 3-tile radius at the end of each round.

---

## Enemies

### Regular Robot
Basic enemy with ranged attacks and moderate damage.  

### Road Wheel Robot
Stronger version of the regular robot with shotgun-like attacks.  

### Cybertruck
Fast and durable. Can run over units and explodes on contact with defenses.  
Spawns additional enemies upon destruction.  

### Spawner
Summons groups of weaker robots over time. Dangerous in large numbers.  

---

## Notes

- Strategy and placement are key to survival  
- Different enemies require different counters  
- Upgrade and adapt your defenses each round
